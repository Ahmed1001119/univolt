About the Project

The Universal PDM is a product developed by Electrical Engineers at Rochester's Institute of Technology Dubai. Designed to replace traditional fuses and relays, this module simplifies vehicle wiring, improves reliability, and enables advanced diagnostics.

Key Features

  • Solid-state switching with MOSFETs
  • FPGA-based logic control (Intel DE10-Nano)
  • Modular & scalable design
  • Built-in current sensing and diagnostics
  • Supports 12V and 48V automotive systems
  • Optional CAN bus & IoT extensions
